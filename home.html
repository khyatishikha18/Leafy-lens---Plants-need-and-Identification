🌿 About Leafy Lens

Leafy Lens is an AI-powered Plant Identification and Plant Need Recommendation System developed to identify plants from leaf images and provide complete information about their care and growth. The system provides details such as scientific name, water requirement, sunlight requirement, soil type, fertilizer recommendation, temperature, plant care guide, health status and disease information to help users maintain healthy plants.
