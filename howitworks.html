❓ How Leafy Lens Works

Follow these simple steps to identify your plant and know its requirements.


📷 Step 1

Click on Identify Plant from the navigation bar and upload a clear image of the plant leaf.

🤖 Step 2

Our AI model analyzes the uploaded image and identifies the plant species.

🌿 Step 3

The system provides complete plant information including water, sunlight, soil, temperature and care requirements.

❤️ Step 4

If the plant has any visible disease, Leafy Lens also suggests its possible disease and treatment.



💡 Tips


  • Upload a clear image of the leaf.
  • Use natural lighting.
  • Capture only one leaf at a time.
  • Avoid blurry images.
  • Keep the leaf centered in the image.
